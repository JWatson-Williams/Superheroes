SUPERHEROES

Their origin stories

Spiderman

Spider-man is a Marvel superhero created by Stan Lee. Before he was Spider-Man, he was just regular degular Peter Parker. He was a high school kid who went to go see a demo of some radioactive technology and got bit by a radioactive spider. He gained super strength, "Spidey-Sense," and the ability to shoot webs.

Wolverine

Wolverine is a superhero and a member of the X-men; his origin story is very wild. He was born as James Howlett to John and Elizabeth Howlett; his real dad is their groundskeeper, Thomas Logan. Thomas tried to commit SA on the Howlett's other son, Dog, then killed John Howlett. James then kills Thomas with his bone claws, fled with his childhood companion, Rose, then accidentally kills her too with his bone claws. He then got placed in a circus, killed some more people, then went to reside with the Blackfoot people. Eventually, he joins team X and his story as Wolverine beigns.

The Hulk

Born Bruce Banner, he was a scientist who was present for the first underground trial detonation of the gamma bomb. A teenager named Rick Jones broke in and entered the testing zone. Banner risked his life and saved the teen but as a result, got hit with a crazy amount of gamma. He first changed into a grey skinned verion of the Hulk but over time, turned into a green monster. Banner transforms into the Hulk when he's angered or gets a rush of adrenaline.

More information about The Hulk

Iron Man

Born Tony Stark, he is a billionaire, technology genius who took over his father company at 21. During a testing of new technology in Vietnam, Tony was hit by a piece of shrapnel from a booby trap. The shrapnel was lodged near his heart and without help, Tony would die. He got imprisoned with a well-known physicist, Professor Ho Yinsen, and made the Iron Man suit. He escaped and began to use his suit to help humanity.

Captain-America

Born Steve Rodgers, he was originally a young-lad who really wanted to serve in the military but was deemed unfit. He volunteered for the super soldier project and was injected with the super soldier serum. As a result he became a human in peak physical condition. He was then given the name "Captain America" and worked alongside Bucky to battle Nazi Germany.
