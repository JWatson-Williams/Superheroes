SUPERHEROES

What's a Hero Without Their Powers?

Captain America

Captain America Possesses Superhuman:

  • Agility
  • Stamina
  • Durability
  • Reflexes
  • Strength

He is a master of hand-to-hand fighting and is a skilled military leader and strategist. He also possesses accelerated healing.

Iron-Man

Tony Stark himself doesn't have any powers. However, when he's in his suit he gains access to:

  • Flight
  • Superhuman Strength and Durability
  • A vast array of weapons

Different suits give him access to different abilities. For example, his Hulkbuster armor gives him access to hulk-like strength and durability.

The Hulk

The Hulks abilities are all reliant on his insane strength. His powers include:

  • Superhuman:
    • Strength
    • Durability
    • Healing
  • Jump extreme distances
  • The ability to get stronger the more angry he becomes.

Spider-Man

Spider-Man has all of the abilities of a Spider proportional to him. This includes:

  • Superhuman:
    • Strength
    • Durability
    • Speed
    • Reflexes
    • Healing
  • Spidey-Sense
  • Wall Crawling
  • Web-shooting

Wolverine

Wolverine is one of the most powerful mutants around. His powers include:

  • Superhuman:
    • Strength
    • Reflexes
    • Durability
  • Enhanced senses
  • Enhanced tracking abilities

Wolverine also possesses one of the most powerful healing factors in all of Marvel. It allows him to regenerate quickly and slows his aging. In addition, his samurai training grants him excellent fighting ability.
